Traders Alloy application for tradeing & stock market

48h Hackathon design solution for mobile application by Traders Alloy that complements their desktop web application

Team members : Ana Krstevska, Meri Mondovska, Simona Gjosheva, Gorjan Spasev & Petar Popovski

Task

During customer interviews, the stakeholders have identified the need for a mobile version of Traders Alloy. Which will complement their desktop web application by offering their users a workspace for their stock investments when they are out and about or at their day job. Apps in the investment space are usually complicated and not pleasant to use. Therefore, they needed help to find a user interface as well as a user experience that facilitates investment life when you are away from your computer and is a pleasure to use.

Challenge

• The first challenge that we faced for this assignment was that most of the team members that worked on this project met for the first time right before the hackathon beginning

• The second challenge was that only one of the team members had previous experience and knowledge in trades and stock market

Research
• We made deep research and learned much stuff about the stock market and the way stock market apps work, their features and options they need to have and provide. Through the competitive analysis, we concluded few things for the design.
• We interviewed 10 users that matched the target group characteristics and gathered their answers to create the user's goals and frustrations.
